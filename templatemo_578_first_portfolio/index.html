Academic profile

Baimatayeva Sholpan is an Associate Professor with a background in information technology, automation, engineering cybernetics and computerized information handling systems.

Her academic work includes lecturing, university public work, supervision of graduation papers and master's degree candidates, and research in operator training systems based on heat power plant simulators.
